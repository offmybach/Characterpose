A Note to Educators

Clarence Gets a Bargain was designed to make financial literacy accessible and fun for young learners. Each chapter introduces a core money concept through story-driven scenarios that kids naturally engage with. Use it in classrooms, at home, or anywhere curiosity meets a price tag.

About the Author

Born from a passion for giving kids a head start on real-world money skills, this book bridges the gap between play and education. Because every kid deserves to know the value of a dollar before they spend one.
